Questions you're probably about to ask

Honest answers.

I'm not technical at all. Can I actually do this?

Yes — that's the entire point. The "technical" part (installing the AI, connecting GitHub, setting up hosting and your domain) is our job. You watch us do it once. After that, you only ever do one thing: open a window on your laptop and type what you want changed. If you can write an email, you can edit your site.

What does "you own everything" actually mean?

Your code lives in a GitHub repo in your name. Your site is hosted on a Vercel account in your name. Your domain is registered to you with your billing details. We never gate access to any of it. If you decided tomorrow that you never wanted to work with us again, your site keeps working, you keep editing, and there's nothing for us to "take back."

What happens if the AI breaks something?

For the first 30 days, just email us — we'll fix it free. After that, you have two options: pick up the retainer and we handle it, or pay us an hourly rescue rate (currently $120/hr) for the rare big oops. In practice, the AI is very cautious about destructive changes, and your site is automatically backed up every time it deploys, so "rolling back" is usually a one-line ask.

Are there any ongoing costs you don't mention?

Two real ones, both paid directly to the providers (not to us): your domain (~$15/year) and, if your site gets unusually heavy traffic, Vercel hosting (free for the vast majority of solopreneur sites). Most clients pay $15 a year and that's it.

Why not just use Squarespace or Wix?

Two reasons. First: rental. You'll pay $300–$600 every year forever, and the day you stop paying, your site disappears. Second: ceiling. Templates look like templates. The minute you want something they don't offer, you're stuck. Our build is custom from the start, and the AI can change literally anything.

What if I don't have copy or photos?

We'll draft copy with you on the discovery call and refine it together. For photos: a mix of (a) your existing brand photos, (b) carefully chosen stock, and (c) AI-generated imagery if it fits your brand. We'll figure it out without making you spend a weekend writing your own About page.

How is this different from Lovable, Bolt, or v0?

Those tools are great for engineers and tinkerers — but they assume you know how to deploy, connect a domain, and wrangle a codebase when something goes sideways. We do all of that for you, and we set up the editor to live on your laptop with full context about your site. You don't have to learn a new app. You just talk.

Can I add a blog / store / booking page later?

Yes. Blogs and booking embeds are easy and usually free. Full e-commerce is doable but adds complexity — we'll quote it separately if and when you want it. The whole point of how we set you up is that adding things later is just a conversation.
